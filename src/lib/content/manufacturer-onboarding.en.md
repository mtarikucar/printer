# Manufacturer Partnership Overview

You are applying to the Figurine Studio manufacturer network. Please confirm
you have read and accepted the terms below. Once your account is approved,
your panel becomes active and orders will start being assigned to you.

## How it works

- Customers create figurine orders on our portal and complete payment there.
- Our admin team assigns orders based on current workload, location, and past
  performance. You're expected to **accept or reject** the assigned order
  within **24 hours**; otherwise it automatically reassigns to another partner.
- Average production target: **5 business days**. Shipping is initiated by us
  through Yurtiçi Kargo; the label appears in your panel once you mark the
  order "Ready".

## Quality expectations

- Resin print layer height 50 microns or finer.
- Paint and final touch-up reviewed before shipping; cracks, support marks,
  and resin whitening are rejection reasons.
- Each package must include our standard brush set (supplied free) and the
  QR card.

## Payment and invoicing

- Net manufacturer payout is wired to the IBAN you register.
- Payouts are processed weekly on Fridays; a month-end statement is available
  for download in your panel.
- A tax number (VKN or TCKN) and IBAN are required.

## Communication and notifications

- Order assignments, status changes, and admin messages go to your registered
  email address. Please monitor it.
- For urgent contact, the phone number (and optional WhatsApp number) you
  provide are visible to our admin team.

## Account status

- **Pending Approval**: Application under admin review.
- **Active**: Eligible to receive orders.
- **Suspended**: Existing orders continue; no new assignments.

You can pause assignments at any time from your profile page by toggling
"Accepting Orders" off.
